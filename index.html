Our Heritage

Located in the heart of historic Lancaster, Kentucky, Lancaster Baptist Tabernacle stands as a beacon of faith. We are an Independent Baptist Church, dedicated to the King James Bible and the old-time way. Our mission is to love God, love people, and serve our community.

Whether you have lived in Garrard County all your life or are just passing through, you have a place here.
